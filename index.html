Who We Are

Bricks made by hand, tradition & a little stubbornness

For over two decades, Rao Bricks Udhyog has been shaping clay into surfaces that age gracefully — developing depth, character and visual integrity over time. From our kilns in Jhunjhunu to homes, restaurants and institutions across Rajasthan, every brick carries the mark of careful craft.

We manufacture Traditional, Linear and Extruded facing bricks across Classic Reds, Multies and Darks finishes — built for architects who won't compromise on texture.
